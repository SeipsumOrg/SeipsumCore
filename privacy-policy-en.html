Privacy Policy

Seipsum processes personal data only for the purpose of handling inquiries and providing cognitive clarification services.

1. Data Collected

The following data may be collected through the form on this website:

  • email address
  • message content submitted through forms
  • declared level of complexity (if provided)

2. Purpose of Processing

The data is used exclusively to respond to requests for cognitive clarification services and to facilitate communication between the user and Seipsum.

3. Third-Party Processing

The data is processed through the Formspree service, a third-party form processing provider.

Forms data is transmitted through this service to the following contact address: contact@seipsum.org

4. Data Storage

The data submitted through this form is kept for as long as necessary to communicate with the user and manage the request sent, but no longer than 12 months from the last interaction, except in cases where the law requires a different retention period.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

Data is not shared with third parties except when required for technical infrastructure (e.g. form handling services or hosting providers).

6. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect data against unauthorised access, alteration, or loss.

However, no internet-based system can be guaranteed as fully secure.

Users are responsible for their own device and connection security when interacting with the website.

7. User Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their personal data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

8. Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy:

info@seipsum.org
