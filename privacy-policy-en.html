Privacy Policy

Seipsum processes personal data only for the purpose of handling inquiries and providing cognitive clarification services.

1. Data Collected

The following data may be collected through the form on this website:

  • email address
  • message content submitted through forms
  • declared level of complexity (if provided)

2. Purpose of Processing

The data is used exclusively to respond to requests for cognitive clarification services and to facilitate communication between the user and Seipsum.

3. Third-Party Processing

The data is processed through the Formspree service, a third-party form processing provider.

Forms data is transmitted through this service to the following contact address: contact@seipsum.org

4. Data Storage

The data submitted through this form is kept for as long as necessary to communicate with the user and manage the request sent, but no longer than 12 months from the last interaction, except in cases where the law requires a different retention period.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

Data is not shared with third parties except when required for technical infrastructure (e.g. form handling services or hosting providers).

6. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect data against unauthorised access, alteration, or loss.

However, no internet-based system can be guaranteed as fully secure.

Users are responsible for their own device and connection security when interacting with the website.

7. Analytics and Cookies

Seipsum uses minimal analytics technologies to better understand how the website is used and to improve clarity, structure, and user experience.

These technologies may collect limited technical and behavioural information such as:

  • page visits
  • approximate time spent on pages
  • device type
  • general geographic region
  • scroll and interaction patterns

This data is used exclusively for analytical and structural improvement purposes and is not sold or used for advertising profiling.

Analytics technologies may include services such as Google Analytics or similar privacy-oriented tools.

Users may disable cookies through their browser settings at any time.

8. Consent

By continuing to use this website and accepting the analytics notice, users consent to the limited use of analytics technologies as described in this Privacy Policy.

Users may withdraw consent at any time by disabling cookies in their browser settings.

9. International Data Transfers

Some third-party technical providers used by Seipsum may process data outside the user's country of residence.

Where applicable, reasonable safeguards are used to ensure data protection consistent with applicable privacy regulations.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect operational, legal, or structural changes to the Seipsum project.

The latest version will always be available on this page.

11. Data Requests

Users may contact Seipsum regarding:

  • data access requests
  • data correction requests
  • data deletion requests
